SnapShots 1.4

iPhone
iTunes Link

Comments

This is probably one of the most prolific apps I've been involved in. It was featured on the iTunes store for 2 weeks next to Instagram. Built with love and high standards. The project took around 3 months of work, but then there was a redesign that added about 3 more months. I did most of the front end work, and this was one of the most feature rich apps I've worked on. However, this was in 2013 so you won't see the version I worked on though some of my code probably still exists in the latest app. It's also a shame I don't have more images.

Features

  • REST API

  • Likes & Comments
  • Social Media Sharing

Product Description

Snapshots is the only app that captures every wedding moment in one extraordinary gallery. Thousands of professional shots and guest pics come together to tell the whole story of one very special day.

Wedding guests, help your favorite couple capture all those special moments. Download today and get snapping!
