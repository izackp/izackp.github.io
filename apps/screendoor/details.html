Product Description

“One-click” simplicity goes beyond an Amazon cart. Welcome to playdates, 2.0…

  • Kat Gordon, Maternal Instinct

Looking to arrange a playdate today?

Do your kids want to play with friends right now?

You and every parent you know have the same challenge. We have the solution!

ScreenDoor easily allows you to see, within a secure private network, which of your child’s friends are available to play…right now. See all the open ScreenDoors in your personal, extended “neighborhood” and get the kids together!

3 Easy Steps

A. Check to see what other kids in you circle are available to play

B. Make your child available in one-click

C. Easily contact the other parents to coordinate for fun!

As parents, we spend a LOT of time trying to manage our children’s social calendar and chasing information. We do it through hit or miss tactics - long email chains with lots of parents CC’d, texts - hoping that one of the other kids in the neighborhood will be available to play, etc.

At Uwithus, we’re parents too and we started this company to make our lives, and yours, easier. ScreenDoor is just the beginning. We think by providing better information and tools to help you coordinate with the other parents in your circle, we can make life a lot easier for you and a lot more fun for your kids.

To learn more about ScreenDoor, visit us at www.yourscreendoor.com or send feedback and questions to info@uwithus.com.

Uwithus - happy kids, happier parents!
