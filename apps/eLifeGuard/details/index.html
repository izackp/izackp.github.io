Product Description

Under the corporate entity Seminole Behavioral Healthcare, we have developed a unique smart phone application called eLifeGuard. The primary goal behind this innovation application is to revolutionize the way the community can access their local behavioral healthcare services. eLifeGuard’s features provide total and immediate access to healthcare and behavioral healthcare prevention, and early intervention services for individuals facing an emergency, or placing a mere inquiry. eLifeGuard will direct users to skilled clinicians available 24⁄7, on a county-by-county basis. The clinicians will assess and navigate the user to the healthcare and behavioral healthcare services, and additional supports that they may need. eLifeGuard is here to change the way view mental illness by providing the insight that one is usually too apprehensive or scared to venture out and find on their own. Finally there is a device that provides assistance from the comfort of your own home. Features: · Diagnostic Tools · Quick & easy assessment · Geo-locating Facilities near you · 24⁄7 Access to Trained Clinicians · Depression, Substance Abuse & Interpersonal resources & tips · Emergency Response Capabilities Disclaimer: We currently have resources listed for SEMINOLE COUNTY FLORIDA ONLY, but are working hard to bring more resources to the app very soon
