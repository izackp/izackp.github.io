Product Description

Thanks for downloading HPM! We’re currently working on fixing some bugs caused by iOS 9. Thanks for your patience. A new version will be available soon! Now you can keep images and videos of your child’s daily homeschool activities in one organized file. Simply take a photo or video of your child’s project, tag it with the date and subject and any additional notes, and save! With HPM, you’ll stay organized, you’ll keep amazing records, and you’ll have your child’s entire school year at your fingertips!

Features:

  • Keep your child’s work and all your memories organized for the entire school year

  • Easily scroll through your child’s work in any subject

  • Set the frequency of your entries in each subject. Let HPM remind you when you need to make another entry!?

  • Customize your child’s subjects and activities so you can enter any topic your child is studying or any extracurricular activity, sporting event, or hobby.

  • Compile your child’s Reading List using photos or Amazon search

-With the iCloud, you can backup your child’s work and access HPM on all your apple products!
